Baithakji:

Shri Vallabhacharya's journey through India

Vallabhacharya gave discourses on Bhagavat at 84 locations around India. They are known as Chaursi Baithak ( ).

Vallabhacharya was a 15th-century philosopher who founded Pushti Marg, a path of devotion within the Hindu Bhakti movement. He was born in Rajasthan, studied in Varanasi, and went on a pilgrimage across India before settling in Mathura. There, he established a temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and taught the importance of bhakti and divine grace. Today, he is revered as a saint and philosopher, and his followers continue to practice the Pushti Marg (path). Visit Nathdwara.in.
